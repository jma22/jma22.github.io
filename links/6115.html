Summary

I go into more details in the paper but here's the gist of the project

Music

By hardcoding the frequency and duration of notes, I used an opamp and a DAC connected to the PSoC stick to play a version of Everytime we touch.

Input

By putting foil on socks and the floor, I created makeshift switches which are connected to the intel 8051, it then debounces and detects state changes.

Display/Game Engine

The PSOC acts as an engine, taking in inputs and interupts from the 8051 and sends interupts to the PSoc Stick (music). It also displays the game on a tft display. This way the music and input sensing does not affect the main gameplay loop.
