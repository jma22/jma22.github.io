Summary

What

  1. Simulated burning away of mesh by maintaining a queue of burnable traingles - similar to BFS
  2. Used Navier Stokes to simulate realistic motion of flame particles along with RK-4 integrator
  3. Track innermost zone of flame with a running average
  4. Use innermost zone and lifetime of particles to determine realistic color of particles
  5. Use alpha blending to simulate particle fading

Takeaway

Real time combustion achieved - no pre rendering needed
