Description

Summary

A unique version of a multiplayer deathmatch rhythm game between three players battling for dominance of their own music line.

Controls

Players use their mouse to aim in a direction and bullets are fired at set points of the song, this bullet then propels the player backwards. This is the only method of attack and movement.

Musicality

Everyone starts out without sound and only the percussion rhythm-- the more rounds you win, the louder your track gets. Picking up powerups not only gives you advantages like extra life, speed, or more damage, but also changes your melody -- adding chords, pedal or reverb effects, or doubling the notes
