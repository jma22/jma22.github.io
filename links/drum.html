Taiko Drum

tldr;

I speedrun a taiko drum at MakeMIT with my friends.

Ideas

At first we thought about using stuff like accelerometers/capacitors to sense drum hits. But then we resorted to the basics - aluminum foil.

Creation

So by layering 4 different pieces of aluminum foil with some tape, we effectively created 4 buttons, that can be closed by hitting the drumstick (coated with foil). After some debouncing and flashing to arduino as a keyboard. We were able to play taiko with the drum on our laptops!
